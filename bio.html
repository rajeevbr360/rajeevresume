Rajeev BR - Bio

Greetings! I'm Rajeev, a dedicated software engineer with a passion for web development and artificial intelligence. Born and raised in Bangalore, India, I've always been fascinated by the power of technology to solve complex problems and improve lives.

My journey in the world of computer science began at the National Institute of Technology, Trichy, where I earned my Bachelor's degree. Eager to delve deeper into advanced computing concepts, I pursued my Master's in Computer Science at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Bangalore.

When I'm not immersed in code or exploring the latest developments in AI, you can find me trekking in the Western Ghats, practicing yoga, or volunteering at local STEM education initiatives to inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts.
